# AI Customer Support Email Responder
## Version 1.3

Status: ✅ Stable Release

---

# Overview

Version 1.3 introduces Customer Relationship Management (CRM) logging using Google Sheets.

Every customer interaction is automatically recorded, allowing support teams to track requests, monitor customer history, and manage support operations efficiently.

---

# New Features

## CRM Logging

Automatically logs:

- Timestamp
- Ticket ID
- Customer Email
- Customer Name
- Email Subject
- Issue Category
- Priority
- AI Confidence
- Ticket Status
- AI Reply
- Escalation Status
- Assigned Team
- Internal Notes

---

## Google Sheets Integration

Customer data is automatically stored inside Google Sheets without manual intervention.

Each support request creates a new CRM record.
