# AI Customer Support Email Responder
## Human Escalation

Status: ✅ Implemented

---

# Overview

Some customer requests require manual review instead of an automated AI response.

The workflow automatically detects these situations and flags them for the customer support team.

---

# Escalation Examples

- Complex refund requests
- Policy exceptions
- Customer complaints
- Requests requiring manual verification

---

# Workflow

Customer Email

↓

AI Classification

↓

Escalation Check

↓

Support Team Assignment

↓

CRM Update

---

# Benefits

- Prevents incorrect AI decisions
- Improves customer satisfaction
- Allows human review when necessary
- Safer customer support workflow
