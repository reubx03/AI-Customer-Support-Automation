# Damaged Product Policy

## Eligible Cases

Customers may report a damaged product if:

- The item arrived broken.
- The item arrived cracked.
- The product is defective upon arrival.
- Parts are missing from the package.
- The packaging was heavily damaged during delivery.

---

## Information Required

Support agents should request:

- Order Number
- Email used to place the order
- Clear photos of the damaged product
- Photos of the packaging
- Photos of the shipping label (if available)

---

## Review Process

Every damage claim must be reviewed before approval.

Support agents should verify:

- The reported damage.
- The order details.
- Supporting images.

---

## Resolution Options

Depending on the investigation, NovaCart may offer:

- Product replacement
- Refund
- Store credit

The final decision depends on the review outcome.

---

## Non-Eligible Cases

Claims may be declined if:

- Damage resulted from customer misuse.
- Normal wear and tear is reported.
- Required evidence is not provided.
- The report is submitted outside the support window.

---

## Customer Communication

Support should:

- Acknowledge the customer's frustration.
- Request the required evidence politely.
- Never promise an immediate replacement or refund.
- Explain the review process clearly.
