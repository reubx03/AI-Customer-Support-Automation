# General FAQ

## What payment methods do you accept?

NovaCart accepts:

- Credit Cards
- Debit Cards
- UPI
- Net Banking
- Popular Digital Wallets

Payment methods may vary by region.

---

## Can I change my shipping address?

Customers can request an address change only before the order has been shipped.

Once shipped, the address usually cannot be changed.

---

## Can I change my order?

Orders can only be modified before shipment.

After shipment, changes are not possible.

---

## Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, NovaCart ships only to supported domestic delivery locations.

International shipping is not available.

---

## How can I contact customer support?

Customers can contact NovaCart Customer Support by replying to the support email.

Support agents will respond as soon as possible during business hours.

---

## Business Hours

Customer Support is available:

Monday – Friday

9:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Public holidays may affect response times.

---

## How long does it take to receive a response?

Most customer inquiries receive a reply within one business day.

---

## Can I return an item?

Returns are accepted only for eligible products according to the Refund Policy.

Support will guide customers through the return process after reviewing their request.

---

## Do you offer store credit?

Yes.

Where applicable, customers may choose store credit instead of a refund.

---

## Can I track my order?

Yes.

Customers receive a tracking number and tracking link after the order has been shipped.

Tracking information may take up to 24 hours to become available.
