# Refund Policy

## Refund Eligibility

Customers are eligible for a refund if:

- The product arrives damaged.
- The wrong item was delivered.
- The product is defective.
- The order is cancelled before shipment.
- The package is confirmed lost by the courier.

Refunds are not available for customer misuse or normal wear and tear.

---

## Refund Request Requirements

Support agents should request:

- Order Number
- Email used to place the order
- Photos of the product (if applicable)
- Photos of the packaging (if damaged)
- Brief explanation of the issue

---

## Refund Review Process

Every refund request is reviewed before approval.

Support agents must never promise an immediate refund.

---

## Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used during purchase.

Store credit may be offered if the customer prefers.

---

## Refund Processing Time

After approval:

- Card payments: 5–10 business days
- UPI payments: 2–5 business days
- Wallet refunds: 1–3 business days

Processing times may vary depending on the payment provider.

---

## Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will normally not be approved when:

- The product shows signs of misuse.
- The customer changes their mind after using the product.
- The request is outside the refund window.
- Required evidence is not provided.

---

## Customer Communication

Support should:

- Show empathy.
- Explain the review process clearly.
- Never promise approval before verification.
- Inform customers about expected timelines.
