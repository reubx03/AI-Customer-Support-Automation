# Shipping Policy

## Processing Time

- Orders are processed within 1–2 business days.
- Orders placed on weekends or public holidays are processed on the next business day.
- Customers receive an order confirmation email immediately after placing an order.

---

## Shipping Time

Estimated delivery times:

| Region | Delivery Time |
|---------|---------------|
| Local | 2–5 Business Days |
| National | 4–8 Business Days |
| Remote Areas | 7–12 Business Days |

Delivery estimates may change due to weather, courier delays, or public holidays.

---

## Tracking Orders

Customers receive a shipping confirmation email containing:

- Tracking Number
- Courier Name
- Tracking Link

Tracking information may take up to 24 hours to appear after shipment.

---

## Delayed Shipments

If a package has not arrived within the estimated delivery window:

- Verify the shipping address.
- Check the tracking link.
- Contact NovaCart Customer Support.

Support agents should request:

- Order Number
- Tracking Number
- Email used for purchase

Agents should never promise an exact delivery date unless confirmed by the courier.

---

## Lost Packages

A package is considered lost if:

- Tracking has not updated for more than 7 business days.
- The courier confirms the shipment cannot be located.

Support should investigate with the courier before offering compensation.

---

## Incorrect Address

If the customer entered the wrong shipping address:

- Address changes are possible only before shipment.
- Once shipped, the address usually cannot be modified.

---

## Shipping Restrictions

NovaCart currently ships only to supported delivery regions listed during checkout.

International shipping is unavailable.

---

## Customer Communication

Support should:

- Be empathetic.
- Never blame the courier.
- Never guarantee delivery dates.
- Keep customers informed throughout the investigation.
